1. Plank Era (10 Planks - 1 Particle)

1.1 Before Sacrifice (10 Planks - 1e13 Planks)

You will start with 10 Planks. With these planks, you can buy a Plank Generator 1.

There are 4 columns of numbers. They represent these informations: (from left to right) name, quantity, grow speed, and multiplier.

when you buy a generator, the multiplier will *1.05, and its cost will *3number.

Keep going to get more planks. When you get 1e13 planks, you can sacrifice.
